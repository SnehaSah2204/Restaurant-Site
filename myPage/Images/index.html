About Us

Since 1965

Perfectly placed between the Opéra Garnier and Place Vendôme, Bienvenue a la Maison has been welcoming guests for almost 50 years now. This classic restaurant ensures the finest dining experience.With a tradition of excellence, guests can expect to find personalised service along with delicious contrast of past and present,well demonstrated by the rich interior of Duke’s Bar and innovative menu of the Resturant. A highlight of the resturant has to be the 180 degree open air dining with a view of the entire city
